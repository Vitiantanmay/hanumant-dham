Hanumant Dham Events

Discover the rich cultural heritage and upcoming events at Hanumant Dham

Upcoming Events

Hanuman Jayanti

Celebration for Lord Hanuman Birthday

Hours: 8:00 AM - 6:00 PM daily

The 84 Pillar Cenotaph is an architectural marvel built in the 17th century as a memorial for a Bundi ruler. Each of the 84 pillars features unique carvings depicting scenes from Hindu mythology. The cenotaph is particularly stunning at sunset when the golden light illuminates the intricate stonework.

Navratri

A huge kanya bhojan was organised

Hours: 9:00 AM - 5:00 PM

Sukh Mahal was built as a summer retreat for the royal family, featuring a unique cooling system that used water channels to lower temperatures. The palace is famous for its beautiful frescoes and the legend that Rudyard Kipling wrote part of "Kim" while staying here. The surrounding gardens are perfect for a peaceful stroll.

Maha Shiv Ratri

Wedding of lord Shiva and Mata Parvati

Hours: Included with palace entry

Chitrashala is a breathtaking gallery of murals that showcase the distinctive Bundi school of miniature painting. The walls are covered with vibrant scenes from the life of Krishna, royal processions, and courtly life. The paintings date back to the 17th-18th centuries and have been remarkably well-preserved, retaining their original colors.
